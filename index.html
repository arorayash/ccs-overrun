BACKEND DEVELOPMENT.

Say whatever about VIT, we really like the way they have computerized everything. From FFCS to Room Allotment to VTOP to Fine Collection :P, all the stuff is pretty fast in here.
Although, one thing we think they can improve upon is the collection of luggage during room shifting. That process is painstakingly slow and can easily be rectified via an web app.
You must have had some of these experiences, take a printout of the form, get all luggage calculated, get all your mobile number of friends and carry that small slip all throughout the vacation, etc. Imagine filling all this details beforehand on a website, getting your storing location from that site and just going there with your mobile and luggage and yeah id card, almost forgot. It will also make things much easier for VIT management because they will have full knowledge of amount of stuff which needs to stored beforehand and they don't have maintain all those hefty ledgers.
Think you are up for it? Then get started! If its good, you will get a seat in our web team and maybe VIT might even use your system next sem.

Note : An docker image would be preferred over a github repo.
